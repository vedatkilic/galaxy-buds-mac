Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Galaxy Buds for Mac (the “app”) is a free, open-source macOS application. Your privacy matters, and the app is designed to need none of your data.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account and no sign-in.

No tracking or network use

  • No analytics, tracking, telemetry, or advertising.
  • No third-party SDKs.
  • No network connections. The app communicates only with your Galaxy Buds over Bluetooth, and all data stays on your Mac.

Permissions

The app requests Bluetooth access for the sole purpose of connecting to and controlling your earbuds. No other permissions are requested.

This website

This page is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like any website, the host may keep standard server logs (such as IP addresses) for security and reliability; see GitHub’s privacy statement. This site itself sets no cookies and runs no analytics.
